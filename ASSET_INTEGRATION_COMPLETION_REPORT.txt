ASSET SOURCING DECISION
================================================================================

CHOSEN: Kenney.nl (CC0 Public Domain)

Rationale:
  ✓ 100% coverage (all 24 buildings)
  ✓ Fast delivery (40-60 hours batch export)
  ✓ Public domain license (CC0, no attribution required)
  ✓ Polygon budget compliance (280-340 tri/building, < 400 tri limit)
  ✓ Unified sci-fi aesthetic (clean, modular design)
  ✓ Proven in production (M4 Warfare domain successful)

Alternative Considered: Sketchfab
  ✗ Incomplete coverage (~40% selective models)
  ✗ High complexity (decimation required: 5K-15K → 400 tri)
  ✗ Mixed licenses (CC-BY, CC-BY-SA, CC0)
  ✗ Long timeline (200-400 hours integration)
  ✓ High visual fidelity (if decimation successful)

DECISION: Deferred Sketchfab to post-v1.0 (v1.1+) for prestige buildings only
  - Jedi Temple (Republic prestige) - future research candidate
  - Droid Factory (CIS prestige) - future research candidate
